Rick and Morty Space Invaders

**Objective**

Defeat the invading aliens by shooting them down before they reach the bottom of the screen.

**Controls**

Use the left and right arrow keys to move Rick & Morty horizontally.

Press the 1 key to shoot lasers at the invading aliens.

If an alien reaches the bottom of the screen, you'll lose a life.

**Tips**

Prioritize shooting down the aliens that are closer to the bottom, as they pose a more immediate threat.

Move left and right strategically to maintain a good shooting position.

Have fun kicking some Cromulon A**!
